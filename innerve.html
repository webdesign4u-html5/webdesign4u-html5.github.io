ABOUT US

Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women(IGDTUW).With its inception in 2015, every year ,the two day extravaganza has witnessed phenomenol gathering of students from all domains .The event is back for its sixth edition where it aims to be a platform for participants to test their engineering skills and showcase their prowess.
